Setup Info

To start, two players will input their names into the two player forms. Player one is red, player two is blue.

The goal is to line up 4 squares in a row. This can be done vertically, horizontally, and diagnoally. The first to 4 wins. Standard difficulty setting has no timer for player turns. Timed difficulty sets a timer of 5 seconds for player turns.

There will be some text at the top indicating who's turn it is.
